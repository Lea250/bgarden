The Orchidaceae are a diverse and widespread family of flowering plants, with blooms that are colourful and fragrant, commonly known as the orchid family. Confucius admired the orchid and wrote many poems regarding the exotic flower and compared them to the character of noble scholars. Orchids were the topic of many of his philosophical and literary works, and formed the basis of his moral teachings.
